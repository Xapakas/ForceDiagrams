Datasets That Can Be Represented With a Force Diagram

  1. A dataset containing countries and their neighbors. In this case, nodes could be countries, and edges would exist between countries that border one another. Countries could even be color-coded by continent for extra effect.
  2. A dataset containing colleges and the other colleges their applicants most often applied to. Nodes could be colleges, and the "strength" of an edge would be based upon what percentage of the original college's applicants also applied to the other college.
  3. A dataset concerning celebrity relationships. Nodes could be celebrities who have been in multiple relationships with other celebrities. Edges would imply some relationship, and could be color coded to indicate "married", "dating", "broken up", etc.
